Privacy Statement

The following policy describes the information gathering and dissemination practices for Lumos Consulting, Inc. (hereafter “Lumos”) properties, including the lumoscloud.com and lumosconsultinginc.com website, all sites in the lumoscloud.com and lumosconsultinginc.com domain (app.lumoscloud.com, learning.lumoscloud.com, *.lumoscloud.com, *.lumosconsultinginc.com), and all sites in the Lumos network.

SECTION 1 – Information Gathering

Lumos logs server information related to identify: (1) usage levels of specific areas of our site, (2) origin of our site’s audience, and (3) browsers and operating systems of site visitors. We do not associate IP addresses to individuals. As such, visitor sessions will be tracked, but visitors will remain anonymous.

The following information may be collected for this analysis, including, but not limited to:

  • User client hostname – The hostname (or IP address if DNS is disabled) of the user/client requesting access.
  • HTTP header, “user-agent” – The user-agent information includes the type of browser, its version, and the operating system it is running on.
  • HTTP header, “referrer” – The referrer specifies the page from which the client accessed the current page.
  • System date – The date and time of the user/client request.
  • Full request – The exact request the user/client made.
  • Status – The status code the server returned to the user/client.
  • Content length – The content length, in bytes, of the document sent to the user/client.
  • Method – The request method used.
  • Universal Resource Locator (URL) – The location of a resource on the server.
  • Query string of the URL – Anything after the question mark in a URL.
  • Protocol – The transport protocol and version used.

Other than information voluntarily provided by visitors, Lumos does not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or address).

SECTION 2 – Information Provided Voluntarily

When using any of Lumos' website, you may choose to provide information to us through web forms, email, or other electronic means. Any personally identifiable information you submit will be used only for the purposes indicated. Requests for information will be directed to the appropriate staff and may be recorded to help us improve our site and better respond to similar requests.

SECTION 3 – Use of Information

Lumos uses the information described above to tailor content to visitors, ensure broad access to our site regardless of software, monitor site performance, and generate aggregate statistical reports. At no time do we disclose site usage by individual IP addresses.

Lumos will not sell, exchange, or otherwise distribute your personally identifiable information without your consent, except to the extent required by law. Each web page requesting information discloses the purpose of that information. Persons not wishing to have submitted information used in that manner are not required to provide it.

SECTION 4 – Security

We may disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so or if you violate our Terms of Service.

Payment:

PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of credit card information by our store and its service providers.
