Get to Know Us

Lumos is a different type of technology company. What does that mean? Well, our business is primarily relational. Think of us as a mentor. We equip value-added resellers (VARs), systems integrators (SIs), cloud architects, and DevOps engineers with the wisdom necessary to develop and launch complex cloud infrastructures.

But beyond that, we believe the relationships between people are just as important as the technologies behind the systems. We want to hear you, partner with you, grow you–we want to be an extension of your business. Let us understand your goals so together we can make your project a success.

Our Promise

You are a key part of the industry. You are valuable and needed. We promise to partner with you to grow your skills and your business. Let us help you navigate our fast-changing industry.

Our Experience

We have designed and deployed many successful cloud systems based on a complete business stack of technologies, both for small and medium businesses as well as for Fortune 10 enterprises and large public service providers. We specialize in:

  • CMDB/ITSM/ITOM technologies such as ServiceNow
  • Orchestration technologies such as CliQr and Cisco UCS Director (UCSD)
  • Infrastructure technologies such as Cisco Application Centric Infrastructure (ACI) and Unified Computing System (UCS) and OpenStack Neutron
  • Hypervisor technologies such as VMware ESXi, Microsoft Hyper-V, and OpenStack Nova/KVM

Lumos is embedded within and an extension of Cisco’s Insieme Business Unit, which is responsible for the ACI, Nexus 9000, and 3000 architectures. We also have strategic relationships with CliQr, ServiceNow, Data Torrent, and many other industry leaders. In fact, Lumos Cloud Labs functions as the exclusive proving grounds for a complete business stack of cloud services based on these solutions where they house, test, and validate their code against our multi-faceted, multi-data center and complete cloud architecture.
